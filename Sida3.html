Builds

When building Katarina, focus on maximizing her damage output and survivability. Prioritize items that enhance her burst damage and mobility, such as Shadow Flame, Hextech Gunblade, and Zhonya's Hourglass. Sorcerer's Shoes are a common choice for boots, providing additional magic penetration to amplify her damage. If the enemy team has a lot of attack damage then Plated Steelcaps can be a good choice for boots, while Mercury's Treads can be more effective against teams with heavy crowd control and magic damage. Always adapt your build based on the specific game and enemy team composition to maximize Katarina's effectiveness on the battlefield.
